Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Overview

TargetResume helps users create, tailor, save, and export resumes. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users of the site.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including account details and resume-related content you enter into the app.

  • Name, email address, and login information
  • Profile details such as education, skills, projects, work experience, and certifications
  • Saved resumes, job tracker entries, folders, notes, and exported resume content

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and display your profile, resumes, and job tracker data
  • To generate AI-assisted resume rewrites based on the information you provide
  • To improve site functionality, reliability, and user experience

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand general website usage, such as page visits and feature interaction. Google Analytics may collect information such as pages visited, device/browser information, approximate location, and interaction data. For more information, visit Google’s Privacy Policy.

How Your Data Is Stored

Information you submit may be stored in third-party infrastructure used to run the application, such as database hosting, analytics services, and AI service providers used to process rewrite requests.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers only as needed to operate the app, such as hosting, analytics, and AI processing services.

Data Retention

We keep account and resume-related information for as long as it is needed to provide the service, maintain the application, or meet legal and operational requirements.

Your Choices

You can choose what information to provide in your profile and saved resumes. If you want your account or data removed, you can contact us to request deletion.

Contact
